Questions that keep the work honest.

Is Focus Flow therapy?

No. It can be therapeutic in effect, but it is not therapy, diagnosis, or crisis care. It is a methodology for self-observation, state reading, and embodied choice.

Is it spiritual?

Yes, but not in a decorative way. Soul / Higher Self is part of the map. It does not cancel the body, boundaries, responsibility, or common sense.

Is this another productivity system?

No. Productivity asks how to do more. Focus Flow asks who is acting, from which state, and whether the action is honest.

What if I do not believe in Soul?

Use the word direction, conscience, deeper knowing, or long-range truth. The practice still works as a map of body, mind, meaning, and action.

How often should I practice?

Short and often beats intense and rare. Five honest minutes can be enough to interrupt a whole day of automatic movement.

Can this replace medical or mental health support?

No. If you are in crisis, unsafe, medically unstable, or dealing with severe symptoms, get appropriate professional support.
