dictionary

Terms that carry weight.

BodyMindSoulObserverSelfDisidentificationFocusEmbodied choiceIntegration

Body

The first field of data: sensation, energy, contraction, impulse, boundary, capacity.

Mind

The layer of interpretation, roles, strategy, protection, memory, and narrative.

Soul / Higher Self

The level of meaning, direction, and deeper coherence. Not decoration. Not an excuse to ignore the body.

Observer

The capacity to notice experience without becoming fully fused with it.

Self

A term used in some psychological systems for a stable inner center. In Focus Flow it is not automatically the same as Soul.

Disidentification

The move from “I am this state” to “this state is moving through me.”

Focus

The organizing point of attention. What you feed with attention begins to shape the state.

Embodied choice

A choice that enters the physical world: speech, boundary, rest, movement, repair, decision.

Integration

Repeating a new route until it becomes available under pressure.
