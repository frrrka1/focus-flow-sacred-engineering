the loop

Six moves. No fog.

01

Notice the signal

Before meaning, there is a body signal: pressure, heat, freeze, collapse, impulse, clarity, refusal.

02

Name the level

Is this a body need, a mind program, a soul direction, or an action that has been avoided?

03

Disidentify

“A part of me is afraid” is not the same as “I am fear.” This small distance changes the field.

04

Tell the truth

Not the dramatic truth. The operational truth: what is happening, what is needed, what is no longer honest.

05

Choose the next move

One embodied action: breathe, speak, stop, ask, leave, write, repair, rest, decide.

06

Repeat until it becomes a route

A new life is not built from one revelation. It is built from repeated honest returns.

important distinction

Observer is not Soul.

The Observer creates distance. Soul gives direction. Self can mean a stable center in psychological language. Focus Flow keeps these words separate so the practice stays clean.
